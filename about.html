About us

An undying promise to keep serving sweetness. We have been doing this since 2024 and we are just getting started. From the guardhouse in Uxbridge, we have seen the passion of our founder Ayah Kikiope Adewale become a sensation, and right now we can only dream on. Ayah Kikiope's Cuisine (AKC) is one of the leading brands in the Quick Service Restaurant Industry in UK. AKC was incorporated in 2024 as a private company and strives to operate with integrity and trust to create value for our customers and staff. AKC is not just a recognized name in the Quick Service Restaurant Industry the UK but also a pioneer in introducing African cuisine and intercontinental meals into the QSR sphere. We offer a wide variety of quality and affordable meals in a relaxed ambience. Our signature dishes are our Yam Pottage, Jamboree Rice and Bullah rice. We are your one-stop shop for anything food and fun and have a number of exciting sub-brands including OMG Cakes, Sweet Heart Bread and Funsation
